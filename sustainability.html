CONSCIOUS LUXURY

We believe that true elegance shouldn't cost the earth.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

At Pato Nepal, sustainability is not just a trend; it is the core of our business model. Rooted in the heritage of Kathmandu, we strive to create garments that honor both the artisan and the environment. We move away from fast fashion, focusing instead on timeless pieces designed to last a lifetime.

ETHICAL MATERIALS

We source 100% organic cotton and recycled linens. Our fabrics are chosen for their low environmental impact and high durability.

FAIR PRODUCTION

Every piece is crafted in factories that prioritize worker safety and fair wages. We know the names of the people who stitch our clothes.

COMMUNITY

We donate 2% of our annual profits to local educational initiatives in Nepal, empowering the next generation of artisans.

"The future of fashion is circular. We are committed to closing the loop by 2030."
